Dream Developers Pvt.Ltd.

Dream Developers Pvt.Ltd.is one of the emerging firms established on 17th June 2016 under the Companies Act 2006, section 5, sub-section (1) of Government of Nepal. This private limited company is registered under Department of Small and Cottage Industries.

This consulting company is established by a team of professionals having expertise and long experience in engineering, architecture, technical and socio-economic areas. The company is fully owned by the founders and associates having professional expertise in various related fields including Civil Engineering, Architecture, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Environmental analysis.
