Objectives

  1. Preparation of various Urban and Regional Planning Studies related to urban development, resource mobilization and urban management.
  2. Preparation of Detailed Project Reports for various infrastructure services including buildings, landscapes, bridges, irrigation and roads, along with project management services.
  3. Providing consulting services in the field of technology including supervision, implementation and monitoring of technical and socio-economic projects.
  4. Conducting baseline surveys, monitoring and evaluation of socio-economic studies, need assessment and GIS-based analysis of urban and sub-urban systems.
  5. Preparation of zoning guidelines for urban and peri-urban areas including risk-sensitive land use planning.
  6. Monitoring and evaluation of water and sanitation conditions, environmental assessment and project appraisal.
  7. Preparation of community-based economic development (CBED) project proposals and formulation of business plans.
