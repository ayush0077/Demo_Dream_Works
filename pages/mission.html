Overview

Dream Developers Pvt. Ltd. is an emerging consulting firm established on 17 June 2016 under the Companies Act 2006, Section 5, Sub-section (1) of the Government of Nepal. The company is registered under the Department of Small and Cottage Industries.

The consulting firm was established by a team of professionals with extensive experience and expertise in engineering, architecture, technical, and socio-economic sectors. The company is fully owned by its founders and associates who bring professional knowledge from various fields including Civil Engineering, Architecture, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Environmental Analysis.

Currently, the company employs more than 10 full-time staff members and over 25 professionals working either on a full-time or part-time basis. These include managers, architects, civil engineers, mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, electronics and communication engineers, computer engineers, surveyors, technicians, environmental analysts, and other specialized experts.

Our Mission

Vision: To deliver technology-based, highly effective consulting services for better infrastructure and stronger societies.

Mission: To become one of the leading consulting firms with strong professional ethics, utilizing modern techniques and tools to promote sustainable nation-building.

Strategy

  • To best utilize the knowledge and skills available in the company through encouraging utilization of individual knowledge, skill, and sharing of experience.
  • To enhance the capability of human resources through encouragement, motivation, and appropriate training with exposure to the external environment related to consulting services.
  • To address problems and issues related to the consulting firm through participation in and organization of seminars, workshops, and conferences.
  • To promote ethics and integrity following the standards established by the Nepal Engineering Council and Nepal Engineering Association.
  • To dedicate the firm's efforts toward uplifting the rural and urban economy of the country.
  • To offer the highest quality consultancy services through dedicated professional efforts.
